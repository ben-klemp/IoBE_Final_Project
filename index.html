Importance of Being Earnest is a play written by Oscar Wilde. This play is a farce that pokes fun of the society of the time. It is very satirical and portrays many humorous interactions between the characters. This play follows Jack Worthing and Algernon as they both seek marriage. However, both of them do this under the name of Ernest, causing confusion and contributing to the humorous nature of the play.
